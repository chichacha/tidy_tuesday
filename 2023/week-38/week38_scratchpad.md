quar·to. /ˈkwôrdō/

*noun* **PRINTING**

noun: **quarto**; symbol: **4to**

- a size of book page resulting from folding each printed sheet into
  four leaves (eight pages).

  - a book of quarto size.

    plural noun: **quartos**

  - a size of writing paper, 10 in. × 8 in. (254 × 203 mm).
